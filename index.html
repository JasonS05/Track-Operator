Track Operator

This is a game from the Minecraft mod Steve's Carts 2 for the 1.7.10 version of Minecraft. I do not claim any ownership of the ideas, artwork, design, textures, and so on present in this game. This is simply a recreation of the game on the web for ease of accessibility because I think it's a fun game that should be more accessible to the general public.

This game is designed to be played on a desktop or laptop. Results on mobile devices may vary, and is not guaranteed to work at all.

Instructions:

  • Click on junctions to switch them.
  • Steel junctions (darker colored rails) cannot be manually switched.
  • Detector rails will switch junctions, including steel junctions.
  • Hover over a detector rail to see which junctions it switches.
